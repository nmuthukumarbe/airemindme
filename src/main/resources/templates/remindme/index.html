Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our trial periods, integrations, and setup capabilities.

How does the 21-day free trial work?
You get unlimited, full access to all features under the Growth plan for 21 days. No credit card is required. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time.
Do I need my own WhatsApp Business API?
We handle the official WhatsApp Business setup for you. You don't need any complex engineering or approval processes—it works right out of the box.
Can I send PDF invoices via WhatsApp?
Yes, Numen automatically converts estimates and billing lists into modern PDF invoices and dispatches them straight to your client's WhatsApp.
Does the AI generate messages in Indian languages?
Yes, our AI Generator supports English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati, Marathi, and multiple other regional languages.
Can I import my existing customer list?
Absolutely. You can upload any CSV or Excel sheet, and our intelligent parser will align names, numbers, and due dates immediately.
Are there any setup fees?
No, there are no signup or configuration fees. The setup process is entirely guided and free.
Is my business data secure?
Security is our highest priority. All customer phone numbers, invoices, and transaction metrics are stored securely using database encryption. We run automated daily backups.
Can I cancel or change my plan anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your active subscription directly from your settings dashboard with zero lock-in contracts.
What is the difference between plans?
Plans scale based on the number of active clients you manage. The Growth plan unlocks priority routes, advanced AI template generation, and multi-language support.
How do online booking links work?
Numen gives you a dedicated booking web page. Clients can view open slots, pick styling chairs or diagnostic slots, and receive auto-confirmation alerts on WhatsApp.
